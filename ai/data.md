Why Hohe Warte

## More than a tennis facility

The Hohe Warte is a vibrant meeting place for people who value exercise, nature and community. Personally managed and run with genuine passion for tennis, the club combines sporting quality with a relaxed atmosphere — a place where you feel welcome, on and off the court.

 Well-kept clay courts in summer, an indoor centre in winter and a calendar full of matches, training and tournaments.

Surrounded by the greenery of Döbling — space, fresh air and calm, a small escape from the city.

From the first children's lesson to long summer evenings on the terrace: openness and warmth are part of the game.


## Hohe Warte Tennis Academy

Training with experience and passion: the academy stands for individual coaching in a personal atmosphere — for children, teenagers and adults who want to develop their game with enjoyment and professional guidance. Led by state-certified tennis instructors, with appointments arranged individually for maximum flexibility.
